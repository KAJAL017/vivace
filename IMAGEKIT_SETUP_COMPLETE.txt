🚀 HOW TO USE:

STEP 1: Get ImageKit Credentials
   → Go to https://imagekit.io/
   → Sign up or login
   → Get your credentials from dashboard

STEP 2: Configure in Admin Panel
   → Login to admin panel
   → Go to Settings page
   → Scroll to "ImageKit Configuration"
   → Enter:
      • Public Key
      • Private Key
      • URL Endpoint (https://ik.imagekit.io/your_id)
   → Enable the toggle
   → Click "Save Settings"

STEP 3: Test Upload
   → Go to Products → Create Product
   → Upload images
   → Images will automatically upload to ImageKit!
